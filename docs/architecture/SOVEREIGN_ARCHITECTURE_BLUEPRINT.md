\# SOVEREIGN ARCHITECTURE BLUEPRINT



\## Layer 1 — Runtime



Coordinates the whole Sovereign system.



\## Layer 2 — Command Center



Aggregates revenue, growth, delivery, content, boardroom, learning, strategy and execution.



\## Layer 3 — Executive OS



Includes health, alerts, recovery, autopilot, strategic director, scorecard, mission control and boardroom.



\## Layer 4 — Knowledge OS



Includes knowledge graph, graph intelligence, graph search, executive memory and decision memory.



\## Layer 5 — World Model



Includes world-model, world-model-v2, stress testing, strategic foresight and scenario modeling.



\## Layer 6 — Workflow OS



Includes workflow registry, workflow execution, trigger-based workflows and governed actions.



\## Layer 7 — Creator OS



Includes Publishing Command, Content OS, YouTube Intelligence, social generation and social distribution.



\## Layer 8 — CRM OS



Tracks leads, clients, ministries, partners, subscribers, source, tags, notes and relationship memory.



\## Layer 9 — Revenue OS



Includes revenue intelligence, invoice risk, collection rate, forecast revenue, proposal pipeline and revenue simulation.



\## Layer 10 — Governance



All serious actions pass through approval before execution.

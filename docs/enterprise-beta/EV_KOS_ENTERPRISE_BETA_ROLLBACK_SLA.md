# EV-KOS Enterprise Beta Rollback SLA (EB-9)

EB-9 defines report-only rollback SLA checkpoints for rehearsal safety.

## SLA Targets

- Detect to disable: 5 minutes.
- Disable to stabilize: 15 minutes.
- Tenant recovery: 30 minutes.
- Operator escalation: 10 minutes.

No rollback execution is performed in EB-9.

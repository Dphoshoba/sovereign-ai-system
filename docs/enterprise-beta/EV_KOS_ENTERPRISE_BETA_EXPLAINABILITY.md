# EV-KOS Enterprise Beta Guard Explainability

EB-3 makes guard decisions explainable to operators before enforcement exists.

## Explainability Goals

- Explain why a route is allowed in preview.
- Explain why runtime behavior remains blocked.
- Show missing tenant, workspace, permission, or approval context.
- Provide the next required action.

No AI-generated explanation is used in EB-3. The reasoning is static and
contract-based.

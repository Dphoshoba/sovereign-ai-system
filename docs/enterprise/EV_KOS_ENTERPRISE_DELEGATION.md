# EV-KOS Enterprise Delegation

## Purpose

EA-5 defines delegated review structures without delegated execution.

## Delegation Rules

- Enterprise Administrator to Enterprise Reviewer for review-only work.
- Enterprise Administrator to Research Lead for domain review.
- Enterprise Administrator to Content Strategist for domain review.

## Limits

Delegation cannot approve execution, publishing, graph writes, database writes, OpenAI calls, or auth changes.

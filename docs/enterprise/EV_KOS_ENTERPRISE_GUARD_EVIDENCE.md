# EV-KOS Enterprise Guard Evidence

## Purpose

This document defines the EA-4 guard evidence packet.

## Evidence Types

- Contract evidence.
- Score evidence.
- Constraint evidence.
- Risk evidence.

## Evidence Families

- Tenant isolation.
- Workspace boundary.
- Organization boundary.
- Shared knowledge constraints.
- Cross-tenant risk.

## Boundary

Evidence packets are preview-only. They are not persisted and do not write to Prisma, graph records, telemetry systems, files, or external providers.

# EV-KOS Enterprise Decision Packets

## Purpose

EA-5 defines decision packet previews for future governance.

## Packet Types

- Graph Ingestion Decision Preview.
- Publishing Decision Preview.

## Packet Contents

- Evidence ids.
- Approval chain ids.
- Review workflow step ids.
- Explainability status.
- Explicit non-persistence and non-execution flags.

## Boundary

Decision packets do not approve, reject, execute, publish, persist, write graph data, call OpenAI, or update the database.
